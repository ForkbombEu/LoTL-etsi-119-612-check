## Implementation Notes

- Added namespace-aware direct cardinality/order checks for
  `TrustServiceProviderList`, `TrustServiceProvider`, `TSPInformation`,
  `TSPServices`, `TSPService` and current `ServiceInformation`.
- Applied normative document precedence over Annex C: V2.4.1 clause 5.4.2
  requires `TSPTradeName`, including a locally well-formed official identifier,
  although the pinned XSD still marks the element optional.
- Pinned the 52 V2.4.1 registered service-type URIs as 13 qualified,
  24 non-qualified and 15 nationally defined values. Other absolute URIs remain
  `inconclusive` until registration/meaning evidence is supplied.
- Added multilingual local validation for TSP/service names and definition
  pointers, TSP postal/electronic address checks, official-identifier syntax,
  service supply-point and optional type URI validation.
- Added service-type-aware PKI/non-PKI identity shape checks. PKI tuples require
  an X.509 certificate representation; `/nothavingPKIid` requires one simple
  absolute `Other` URI; explicitly optional families accept either. Custom type
  mode selection remains inconclusive.
- Added EU qualified versus non-qualified/national current-status vocabulary
  checks and strict UTC `StatusStartingTime` ordering against
  `ListIssueDateTime`. Transition/history and signing-time consistency remain
  separate limitations.
- Enforced `TSPServiceDefinitionURI` for `NationalRootCA-QC` and service-level
  definition evidence for `unspecified`; referenced target content is not
  fetched by normal local assessment.
- Added base TSP/service extension wrapper, boolean criticality, EU TSP
  non-criticality and unknown-critical rejection. Detailed predefined extension
  semantics remain TS612-06 work.
- Replaced the old descendant-presence service walker with the exact assessor
  while retaining its indexed legacy finding IDs and certificate evidence.
- Updated ledger coverage to 69 total: 7 implemented, 37 partial and 25 not
  implemented. Coverage gating remains active.

## Known Caveats

- Provider-list absence is conditional on approval history and is
  `inconclusive` without that evidence.
- Formal TSP name/identifier registration and customer-care operation are not
  established from XML syntax.
- Certificate/public-key/SKI equivalence, TSP-name matching, duplicate key use,
  certificate purpose and service-history continuity remain TS612-06 work.
- Status transition legality, initial-status history and signing-time
  consistency require history or signature evidence.
- Definition and supply-point target content is not dereferenced; extension
  subtypes are recognized only for base criticality handling here.
