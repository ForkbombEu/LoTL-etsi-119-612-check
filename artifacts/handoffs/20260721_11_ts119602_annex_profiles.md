## Implementation Notes

- Added a versioned Annex D-I registry containing exact LoTE type, status-determination, scheme-rules, role, service-type, binding, history, pointer, and signature expectations for all six profiles.
- Dispatch uses only the exact embedded `LoTEType`; absent, unregistered, or conflicting embedded/declared profile evidence produces a critical stable `ts119602.profile.dispatch` failure.
- Added stable per-profile findings for `binding`, `scheme_information`, `trusted_entity`, `service`, and `signature`, with structured evidence for every locally decidable rule.
- Implemented UTC calendar-month arithmetic with end-of-month clamping for the six-month profile limit instead of treating six months as 183 days.
- Added profile-specific contact and EU country-role checks, certificate cardinality/name rules, Wallet `ServiceUniqueIdentifier`, Pub-EAA certificate/status/history constraints, registrar supply-point presence, and registered service-type restrictions.
- Preserved the published WRPRC status-determination URI spelling under the existing interpretation identifier rather than silently correcting it.
- Reused the existing JAdES and XAdES evidence and kept profile-signature aggregation separate from local scheme/entity/service outcomes.
- Updated the requirements ledger to 17 implemented, 60 partial, and 4 not-implemented requirement families without claiming complete conformance.

## Known Caveats

- Prior-list sequence progression, self-pointer target authentication, archive/policy dereferencing, distribution equality, authoritative registration claims, F/G certificate-purpose policy, registrar supply-point content authentication, and production trust paths require contextual evidence and remain for TS602-12.
- Official scheme-explicit XML Schema validation and the TS 119 612 alternative XML binding mapping remain incomplete.
- Service types are restricted when present because the profile tables do not uniformly require `ServiceTypeIdentifier` presence.
- Local success cannot produce a complete TS 119 602 `conformant` verdict; `ts119602.coverage.complete` remains `not_checked`.
