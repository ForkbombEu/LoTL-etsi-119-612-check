## Known Caveats

- TS612-02 pins and verifies the bundle but does not change assessment routing.
  Until TS612-03, TS 119 612 validation still requires an explicit `--xsd`
  path and `schema.xsd` remains `not_checked` when none is supplied.
- The official V2.4.1 schema targets
  `http://uri.etsi.org/02231/v2#`. The observed
  `http://uri.etsi.org/19612/v2.4.1#` EUDI RI variant remains compatibility
  evidence and is not silently routed to the normative schema.
- Schema success will remain separate evidence; Annex C gives the normative
  document precedence over electronic-schema conflicts.
- The XAdES schema contains no embedded licence notice, so its provenance
  notice links the ETSI Directives/IPR source instead of asserting that the
  Forge repository licence covers it.
